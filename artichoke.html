Artichoke
RIPE
  • Touch: Heavy and solid. Leaves should squeak when rubbed together. Bend a lea
  • Appearance: Green stem. Thick, tightly closed leaves.
  • AVOID: If there's a brown stem or with open/split leaves or if it begins to flower
